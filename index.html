my fullbody routine

Last updated: July 11, 2025

Legs
Hamstring Curls 2 × 8 · 60s

Keep hips on the pad; avoid using lower back. Control the negative portion of the rep.

Leg Extensions 2 × 8 · 60s

Pause at the top of the movement. Avoid swinging the weight up; focus on quad contraction.

Variations
Squats 3 × 8 · 60‑180s

Keep chest up and back straight. Break at the hips and knees simultaneously.

Back & Biceps
Back Rows 3 × 8 · 60-90s

Pull towards your lower chest, squeezing shoulder blades. Alternate between overhand and underhand grips.

Variations
Lat Pulldowns 3 × 8 · 60-90s

Lead with your elbows and pull the bar to your upper chest. Avoid using momentum.

Hammer Curls + Chin-ups Superset 3 × 8 · 60-180s

For curls, keep elbows pinned to your sides. For chin-ups, use a full range of motion.

Variations
Incline Curls 3 × 8 · 60s

Set bench to an incline. Let arms hang down fully to get a good stretch on the bicep.

Abs (optional)
Hanging Leg Raises 3 × 8 · 60s

Avoid swinging. Engage your core to lift your legs. Keep legs straight for more difficulty.

Variations
Crunches 3 × 8 · 60s

Lift your shoulder blades off the floor. Don't pull on your neck.

Knee Raises 3 × 8 · 60s

Same as leg raises but with bent knees, which is easier for beginners.

Twisting Hanging Leg Raises 3 × 8 · 60s

As you lift your legs, twist your torso to bring your legs to one side to target the obliques.

Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
Cable Chest Press 3 × 8 · 60-90s

Keep shoulders down and back. Focus on squeezing the chest muscles at the peak of the movement.

Variations
Bench Press 3 × 8 · 60-120s

Lower bar to mid-chest. Keep feet planted and don't bounce the bar off your chest.

Incline Bench Press 3 × 8 · 60-120s

Targets the upper chest. Don't let elbows flare too wide.

Tricep Pushdowns + Dips Superset 3 × 8 · 60-180s

Pushdowns: keep elbows tight to your body. Dips: stay upright to target triceps.

Variations
Tricep Extension 3 × 8 · 60s

Can be done with cables or dumbbells. Keep upper arms stationary.

Shoulder Press 3 × 8 · 60s

Press overhead without excessively arching your lower back. Control the weight on the way down.

Lateral Raise 3 × 12 · 60s

Lead with your elbows and raise arms to shoulder height. Avoid using momentum.
